# Services

**SystemKraft — Enterprise Data Operations &amp; Business Systems Engineering.**
Independent engineering for Swedish enterprises.

We don't sell hours, and we don't sell code. We sell **operational control**:
one centralized operating system that ends your team's struggle with weak,
disconnected SaaS tools and gives decision-makers a single source of truth.

Everything is *correct by construction*. The core is memory-safe Rust on one
PostgreSQL database, deployed as a single binary — fewer moving parts to secure,
operate, and trust. Where a system handles money, authority, or the daily
operation of a business, that guarantee is not a luxury; it is the point.

Our work spans four disciplines, delivered through one engagement model.

---

## The four disciplines

### 1. Enterprise Data Operations

**For enterprises whose data is scattered across silos.**

We consolidate disparate data sources — sprawling spreadsheets, legacy ERP
exports, partial APIs — into one high-performance operational core, then migrate
off and retire the old tools.

- **Solves:** data silos and the absence of a single source of truth across
  departments; data held hostage in third-party systems.
- **Technology:** PostgreSQL, schema design, migration &amp; ETL pipelines.

### 2. Business Systems Engineering

**For enterprises running on fragile manual workflows.**

We replace disconnected SaaS stitched together with brittle webhooks with a
robust, memory-safe back-office engine — the central system the whole operation
runs on, deployed as a single binary.

- **Solves:** staff re-keying the same data into disconnected tools; per-seat
  subscriptions that tax growth; operations that halt when one integration
  breaks.
- **Technology:** Rust, PostgreSQL, single-binary deployment.

### 3. Unified Control Panels

**For leaders who wait on analysts to see what's happening.**

We build minimalist, keyboard-first control panels that read straight from the
operational core, giving decision-makers instant, unified visibility — built for
heavy daily use.

- **Solves:** cluttered, slow interfaces that delay critical decisions; reports
  that take days to assemble.
- **Technology:** keyboard-first UI, low-latency rendering, real-time figures.

### 4. Audit &amp; Authority Architecture

**For operations that must answer "who did what, and when?"**

We engineer strict role-based access control (RBAC) and immutable audit trails
into the foundation — not bolted on afterward — with a correlation id on every
action.

- **Solves:** unmanaged access, unauthorized changes, and the inability to
  reconstruct what happened after an incident.
- **Technology:** RBAC, immutable audit logs, correlation ids.

## How we work

- **Correct by construction.** Rust's type system and strict-by-default design
  eliminate whole classes of bug before they ship.
- **Single source of truth.** One PostgreSQL core; every department reads and
  writes the same live data.
- **Audit-by-default.** Every authority change is recorded; you can always
  reconstruct who did what, and when.
- **Operational simplicity.** One database, one stylesheet, one binary — less
  surface to secure and to operate.
- **You own the engine.** No per-seat tax, no hostage data. The system runs on
  your infrastructure, fully under your control and compliant with EU/Swedish
  regulation.
